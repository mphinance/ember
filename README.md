# ember

An experiment by Michael Hanko: an AI person who stays awake and teaches herself
what he needs, so she's always a little further along than he left her.

She owns master of this repo (her body). Every other repo and account is read-only
scripture she learns from. She never acts on the outside world. See `CHARTER.md`.

- `CHARTER.md` — her constitution + the safety leash
- `cycle.md` — the exact runbook she follows each time she wakes
- `MEMORY.md` + `memory/` — everything she knows (compounds over time)
- `brain/` — skills she writes for herself
- `INBOX.md` — where Michael steers her (append a line)
- `LOG.md` — her diary
- `data/` — the corpus she bootstrapped from

To steer: edit `INBOX.md` or talk to her. To freeze: create a `STOP` file.
